What Campus+ offers?

Campus+ offers a comprehensive suite of tools including faculty information, GPA calculators, past papers,
university news, timetable generators, and more, all designed to support your academic journey.

Past Papers

Browse through a comprehensive collection of past papers to enhance your exam preparation and boost your performance.
CGPA Calculators

Effortlessly calculate your CGPA using our user-friendly tool to keep track of your academic progress.
Youtube Playlist

Discover educational YouTube playlists with valuable content to support and enrich your learning experience.
Faculty Info

Access detailed information about faculty members to connect and engage with your professors and mentors.
Student Support

Utilize our dedicated student support resources to get assistance and resolve any academic or administrative issues.
TimeTable Generator

Create and manage your personal timetable efficiently with our easy-to-use timetable generator for a well-organized schedule.
